ARTWORK ABOUT CONTACT

Abel Mndeba

Abel Mndeba is a drawing and painting artist based in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania, born in Mbeya.

His current work focuses on wildlife, especially lion life, reflecting leadership, unity, and social identity.

Through his art, Abel connects animal behavior with human society.
